**The audio controls browsers forgot to include.**

Take complete control of audio in your browser. Boost quiet videos up to 500%, adjust playback speed, seek through media, fine-tune EQ settings, and route tabs to different speakers. Features a real-time audio visualizer, fullscreen video support, and deeply customizable controls. Works on virtually any website.

No subscriptions. No ads. No data collection.

Demo and feature overview at afterbedtimecreations.com/projects
